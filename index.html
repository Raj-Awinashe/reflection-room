About Us

The Reflection Room is a student-led organization at Penn State University Park dedicated to cultivating an entrepreneurial mindset through the power of reflection. Founded in Spring 2025 as a book club from an LDT 100 class taught by Professor Nyandusi Nyachae, our mission is to help individuals grow using their own minds. We believe in the transformative power of introspection and community.

Meet Our Advisor

Nyandusi Nyachae, affectionately known as "Coach Dus," is a World Campus Coordinator and Instructor at Penn State's College of Education. With a passion for empowering students, he guides individuals to discover and manifest their potential. As the advisor of The Reflection Room, he inspires our journey towards personal growth and reflective learning.

He is also the author of Jump Start Your Dreams: How to Discover, Activate & Manifest Your Greatness, a motivational guide that encourages readers to pursue their aspirations with purpose and determination.
